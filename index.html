About

Information today is growing and changing faster than any single person’s ability to manage, access, or effectively use it. How we interact with the expanding universe of information is a challenge that impacts our chances for success every day. Our interface with that information – new devices, new interfaces – is in a constant state of evolution. How we interact and share information with each other is evolving as well. Our connectivity is both a challenge and an opportunity. We can achieve new levels of productivity when accessing relevant information more efficiently and effectively. When we can share our insights effectively with colleagues, our collaboration can enable new levels of innovation.

The thoughtful people at Attensa have been working to simplify and enhance how we access and share information from the ground up. Infogentsia provides the delivery system for the new generation of information and knowledge management tools from Attensa, precision tools for our information abundant world.

Infogentsia was created to simplify information access and sharing. We believe that software needs to be delivered with a high level of effectiveness and service. Whether you’re searching for a solution, need a business partner, or want to join the team, Infogentsia is right for you.
